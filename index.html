Visit Umami in Lefkada

Umami is located in the center of Lefkada Town on Ioannou Mela 165, a short walk from the marina and the main streets.

How to get there

From Lefkada's main waterfront, walk towards the town center and follow the pedestrian street until you reach Ioannou Mela. Umami is a short walk from the marina and the main square, on the corner of Ioannou Mela 165.

If you're driving, follow signs to Lefkada Town, park near the marina or public parking area and continue on foot. You can also use Google Maps directions and navigate straight to the door.
